1.Register a user

Fill in a registration form and register yourself.

Note! You might have to login again after 2 days of inactivity

2 Create a template

Create a list of your expense, filling in the form.

The template will be used for generating the list of expenses for every month.

Note! The timesheet is generated 1/month after choosing the Timesheet option. It is advised to fill in the timesheet with all your expenses, you want to see in this month. Subsequent updates will be seen only the next month!

3 Check your timesheet

After filling in the Template your expenses will be in the actual month's timesheet

Note! If you have skipped the Tempate part your timesheet will be empty. Any change in the Template will be included in the next month's timesheet only!

You can add directly items in your timesheet or delete them. The same is valid for the modification or deletion.

Note! Deletion or modification of items doesn't affect the Template!

4 Update the timesheet

Update the expenses in the timesheet as you pay your them during the month

The following options are available:

  1. - Set Paid immediately, if the planned and paid amounts are equal
  2. - Add an amount to an item, if you pay it more than once
  3. - Modify everything, including the planned amount and name
  4. - Delete item if it has no paiments recorded ( planned = 0 )

5.Check your balance

Check the balance for the current month based on the timesheet records
